About Us

Milky Animal Foundation is dedicated to protecting and rescuing helpless animals across the streets. Whether it's an injured bird, a wounded cow, or a struggling reptile, we ensure they receive the love, care, and treatment they deserve. Our team works day and night to create a safer world for our voiceless friends.
